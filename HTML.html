Cafe Younes

Since 1935, we have been passionate about serving our customers the best coffees from around the world.

Today, after more than 80 years of caffeinated services, we remain committed to our heritage of quality products and special customer service. Café Younes retails high quality coffee that is freshly roasted and ground on premises.
For wholesale orders, we sell ground as well as whole beans, boasting a wide selection of blends made of premium quality Arabica beans. Café Younes introduced its coffee shop line in 2008. In our multiple retail outlets in Lebanon, you can enjoy a cozy break of soothing drinks, a high quality range of homemade desserts, pastries, salads and gourmet sandwiches and burgers along with specialty teas and juices.
